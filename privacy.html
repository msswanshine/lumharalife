Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

At Lumhara, I am committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, store, and protect your information when you visit my website or use my services.

Information I Collect

I collect information that you voluntarily provide to me through:

  • Newsletter Signup: When you subscribe to my newsletter, I collect your email address. This information is stored securely in Google Sheets.
  • Contact Form: When you contact me through the contact form, I collect your name, email address, subject, and message. This information is sent to me via FormSubmit, a third-party form processing service.
  • Website Usage: Like most websites, I may collect certain information automatically, such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This information helps me improve the website experience.

How I Use Your Information

I use the information I collect to:

  • Send you newsletter updates about my work, offerings, and upcoming projects
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Improve my website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

I do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored using the following services:

  • Google Sheets: Newsletter email addresses are stored in a private Google Sheet that only I have access to.
  • FormSubmit: Contact form submissions are processed through FormSubmit and delivered to my email address.

I take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While I strive to protect your personal information, I cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information I hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from my newsletter at any time by contacting me

To exercise these rights, please contact me using the information provided below.

Third-Party Services

My website uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Apps Script: Used to process newsletter signups and store email addresses in Google Sheets
  • FormSubmit: Used to process contact form submissions
  • Google Fonts: Used to display fonts on the website
  • Font Awesome: Used to display icons on the website

These services have their own privacy policies. I encourage you to review them to understand how they handle your information.

Cookies and Tracking

Currently, my website does not use cookies or tracking technologies beyond what is necessary for basic website functionality. If this changes in the future, I will update this policy accordingly.

Children's Privacy

My services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe I have collected information from a child, please contact me immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
