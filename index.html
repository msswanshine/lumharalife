My Approach

Welcome!  My name is Amy Swan, and I'm a Human Systems Guide & Coach. I help people uncover their authentic selves and navigate the world in ways that honor their unique design.

"The privilege of a lifetime is being who you are."

- Joseph Campbell

"I wish I could show you when you are lonely or in darkness the astonishing light of your own being."

- Hafiz

I’ve been interested in personal development for most of my life, but my understanding of it entirely transformed after my first Human Design reading, which helped me see patterns I had been living inside without fully understanding. That experience showed me how much relief can come from understanding how we’re wired rather than trying to fix ourselves. I’m a Certified Life Coach, and my work has been shaped by years of study in human systems and behavior, informed by frameworks such as the Gene Keys, created by Richard Rudd, and mind–body perspectives on belief and biology.


I was raised in a household that held both structured, rational worldviews and more intuitive, exploratory ways of thinking, which naturally shaped my interest in bridging science, systems, and inner experience. Alongside coaching, I bring a background in technology, systems thinking, music, and the practice of aikido (Japanese martial art), all of which inform how I approach growth and change. At the heart of my work is helping people slow down, listen more closely to themselves, and learn to trust the signals already present within them so change can unfold in a grounded and sustainable way.

What Makes This Approach Different

Integrated Human Systems

I uniquely combine Human Design, Gene Keys, Astrology, and Enneagram with neuroscience-backed tools from Neuroplasticity, Meditation, Visualization, and Life Coaching for deeper insights and more effective transformation.

Compassionate Shadow Work

I address all aspects of ourselves, including the "darker parts," with awareness, love, and compassion, resulting in more complete integration and authentic expression.

Inner Guidance First

I don't tell you what to do. Instead, I guide you to find your own answers from within, empowering you to trust your unique way of navigating the world.
